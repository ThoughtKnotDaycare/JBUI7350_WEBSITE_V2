    FAQ's

    Why number 99? How many points does he have? When did he retire?
    Wayne Gretzky originally wanted to wear Gordie Howe's number 9, but when it was unavailible he chose to wear number 99. Wayne ammassed a staggering 2857 points in his 1487 games played. Wayne retired after the 1998-99 season.
